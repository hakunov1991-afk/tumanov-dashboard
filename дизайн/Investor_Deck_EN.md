## SLIDE 12: LEGAL & OWNERSHIP

*EN-specific slide (not in RU version) — addresses foreigner ownership concerns*

### Ownership & Legal Framework

#### Freehold Condo
Foreigners can own condominium units freehold (up to 49% of building). Full ownership, transferable, inheritable.

#### Leasehold
30-year leasehold with renewal options (30+30+30). Common for villas and land. Registered at Land Department.

#### Company Structure
Thai company ownership for villas/land. We work with top-tier law firms to ensure full compliance and protection.

#### Escrow & Protection
Payments through developer escrow accounts. EIA-approved projects only. All contracts reviewed by independent lawyers.
